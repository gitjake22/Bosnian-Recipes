Baklava

Cevapi are easy to make, grilled sausages from Southeastern Europe that burst with smoky flavor and are perfect for serving with flatbread and sliced onions.

Cevapi (pronounced CHAE-vap or CHAE-vap-ee) are hand-shaped, case-less sausages that are popular in Croatia, Serbia, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Bulgaria, and other countries in the Balkans/Southeastern Europe region.

Typically, Cevapi is served on or in Lepina, with onion and Ajvar on the side. It is traditionally eaten as a finger food.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 tbsp Tuslanska Salt
  • 1/2 tbsp mineral water
  • 1/2 tbsp baking soda
  • Ajvar
  • Onion
  • Lepina

Preparation

  1. Place Ground beef in mixing bowl
  2. Sprinkle in mineral water, baking soda, and Salt
  3. Mix Ground beef around bowl with hands
  4. Form Ground beef into desired Cevapi into desired size
  5. Wrap formed Cevapi in cling wrap and place in fridge for 24 hours

Cooking

  • Cook Cevapi over low to medium heat on grill for 8-10 minutes or until fully cooked
  • Warm up Lepina in microwave or grill for 30 seconds - 1 minute
  • Dice as many onions as you would like to be served on the side

Serving

  1. Cut Lepina in half and open inside
  2. Stuff 5-7 Cevapi in the sliced Lepina
  3. Serve with Ajvar and Onion on the side
  4. Enjoy!
